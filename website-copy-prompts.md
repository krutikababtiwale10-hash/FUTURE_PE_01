# Task 1 – AI Website Copy Generator

## Business Type:
Beauty Salon – Glow Studio (Mumbai)

---

### Prompt Strategy:
This prompt is designed to generate structured, conversion-focused homepage content for a beauty salon. 
I included the business name, location, services, tone, and required sections so the AI produces organized and website-ready copy.

## Prompt 1 – Homepage Content

**Prompt:**
Act as a professional website copywriter. Write homepage content for a modern beauty salon named Glow Studio located in Mumbai. The salon offers hair styling, skincare, bridal makeup, and nail art. Tone should be friendly, premium, and welcoming. Include a strong headline, short intro, services highlight, and call to action.

**AI Output:**
**Headline:**  
✨ Glow Like Never Before at Glow Studio

**Intro:**  
Welcome to Glow Studio, Mumbai’s go-to destination for premium beauty and self-care. We blend expert techniques with a luxurious, relaxing environment to help you look and feel your absolute best.

**Our Signature Services:**  
From trend-setting hair styling and rejuvenating skincare to flawless bridal makeup and stunning nail art, our specialists are here to bring your beauty vision to life.

**Why Choose Glow Studio?**  
✔ Experienced beauty professionals  
✔ Premium products & hygienic care  
✔ Personalized beauty consultations  
✔ Warm, relaxing ambiance  

**Call to Action:**  
Ready for your glow-up? Book your appointment today and let our experts pamper you!


### Why This Output Works:
The content includes an attractive headline, clear service highlights, trust-building points, and a strong call-to-action. 
It matches the tone of a modern premium beauty salon and encourages customers to book an appointment.

---
### Prompt Strategy:
This prompt focuses on generating short, persuasive service descriptions for a salon services page. 
I clearly listed each service so the AI produces clean, benefit-focused, and website-ready content.


## Prompt 2 – Services Page Content

**Prompt:**
Write service descriptions for a beauty salon offering haircuts, facials, bridal makeup, and manicure/pedicure. Keep each description short, attractive, and easy to understand.

**AI Output:**
**Haircuts & Styling**  
Get a fresh new look with precision cuts and trendy styles tailored to your personality and face shape.

**Facials & Skincare**  
Rejuvenate your skin with deep-cleansing, hydrating, and glow-boosting facials designed for every skin type.

**Bridal Makeup**  
Look flawless on your special day with long-lasting, camera-ready bridal makeup customized to your style.

**Manicure & Pedicure**  
Pamper your hands and feet with relaxing treatments that leave your nails clean, polished, and beautiful.

### Why This Output Works:
Each service description is simple, appealing, and benefit-focused. 
This makes it easy for customers to understand what the salon offers and helps them feel confident choosing a service.

1. Book your glow-up session today!  
2. Your beauty deserves expert care — reserve your spot now.  
3. Step in stressed, walk out glowing. Book now!  
4. Let us pamper you — schedule your appointment today.
